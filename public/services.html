Our Ayurvedic Services

At Ayupathya, we blend time-honored Ayurvedic knowledge with modern-day health needs. Our services are designed to promote holistic wellness through food and lifestyle guidance tailored to each individual.

Online Consultation

Consult directly with Dr. Pranav Kumar through a secure video platform. Personalized guidance from the comfort of your home.

Offline Consultation

In-person assessments and consultation at our Lakhisarai wellness center. Ideal for comprehensive evaluations.

Customized Diet Plans

Tailored to your dosha (body constitution), health conditions, goals, and Ayurvedic principles.

Workshops & Camps

Group-based sessions, rural outreach programs, and lifestyle workshops that empower communities with Ayurvedic wisdom.

Corporate Wellness

Onsite and virtual programs for employee health, diet planning, and stress management rooted in Ayurveda.

Product Guidance

Guided plans on how to use Ayupathya products as part of your diet and recovery regimen.

Individuals – looking for sustainable wellness through diet and Ayurveda.
