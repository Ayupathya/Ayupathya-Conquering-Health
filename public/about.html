About Ayupathya Aahar Pvt Ltd

Ayupathya Aahar Pvt Ltd, founded in November 2023, is a forward-thinking food and wellness company dedicated to reviving the Ayurvedic way of life. Under the trademark brand name AYUPATHYA and the powerful tagline “CONQUERING HEALTH”, we integrate ancient wisdom with modern food science to create healthy and nourishing solutions for the body and mind.

We offer both offline and online dietary consultancy based on Ayurvedic principles, along with the production of natural food products like Ayupathya Barley Saktuk and Ayupathya Wholesome Daliya, which have been tested and appreciated by local communities.

Our mission is to promote preventive healthcare through personalized Ayurvedic nutrition and traditional dietary habits. We aim to empower every individual with the tools to restore their health naturally.

Our vision is to become a global leader in Ayurvedic food consultancy and traditional product innovation, reshaping modern eating patterns with age-old wellness knowledge.
