About the Class

Our signature program, “Promoting a Healthier Lifestyle with Ayurvedic Diet and Lifestyle”, brings together ancient Ayurvedic wisdom and modern wellness practices. Under the guidance of Dr. Pranav Kumar, this class helps you build a holistic, sustainable lifestyle aligned with your natural constitution.

What You’ll Learn

  1. Fundamentals of Ayurveda: Doshas, elements, and body constitution (Prakriti)
  2. Ayurvedic Diet: Ritucharya, food combinations, and detox principles
  3. Lifestyle Practices: Dinacharya, sleep, stress management, and herbal rituals
  4. Practical Applications: Recipes using Ayupathya products, yoga, and home remedies

Why Choose This Class?

  • Rooted in classical Ayurveda, led by Dr. Pranav Kumar
  • Practical, everyday applications of Ayurvedic wisdom
  • Natural, sustainable, and preventive health guidance
  • Supportive community to grow and learn with

Who Is This For?

  • Individuals seeking natural solutions for better health
  • People dealing with digestive, fatigue, or lifestyle issues
  • Wellness professionals (yoga teachers, nutritionists, etc.)
  • Families looking to adopt Ayurvedic food habits

Outcomes & Benefits

  • Understand your Dosha and balance it effectively
  • Master seasonal and mindful eating habits
  • Learn to prepare Ayurvedic meals at home
  • Improve digestion, immunity, and energy
